Weekend Camping Lists

Listed below are the different kits we use when camping. Each is kept stocked up and ready to go, with the exception of the food kit which is restocked while driving to the camping spot. All the lists below are available to download as a PDF.

Personal

Your personal items include clothing, toiletries, and comfort items like hats.

Sleeping

Sleeping items are the basics, plus anything that might make the evening more enjoyable when surrounded by snoring campmates - like earplugs.

Cooking

The cooking kit includes all the supplies for cooking except the food itself.
