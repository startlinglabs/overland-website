The Science

It's easy to pay a mechanic fix your vehicle. But what happens when your truck breaks down 50 miles from the nearest road?

We help you learn the science behind your vehicle so you are in the best possible possition to:

  • Assess strange noises or catastrophic failures while on the trail.
  • Make field repairs.
  • Avoid modifications to your vehicle that lower the reliability.

Knowing the science behind your vehicle will also make you a more interesting person to talk to and ensure you are a valuable part of any overlanding group.
